## Objectives

SynTemp establishes measurable objectives to support the Quality Policy:

1. On-Time Delivery  
   ≥ 95% of orders delivered on or before committed date

2. Product Conformity  
   ≤ 2% internal nonconforming output rate

3. Customer Satisfaction  
   All customer complaints responded to within 5 business days

4. Internal Audit  
   100% of planned internal audits completed annually

Quality objectives are reviewed during Management Review and updated as necessary.
