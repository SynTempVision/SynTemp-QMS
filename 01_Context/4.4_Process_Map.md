## 4.4 Core Process Flow

The core operational workflow of SynTemp Vision is defined as follows:

1. Customer Order / Request
   - Customer requirements are received and reviewed.

2. Work Order Creation
   - A controlled work order is generated.

3. Material Selection & Procurement
   - Required materials are selected or procured.
   - Materials are verified for suitability.

4. Manufacturing / Fabrication
   - Components are manufactured in accordance with work order requirements.

5. Assembly
   - Components are assembled into finished instrumentation.

6. Testing / Calibration / Verification
   - Products are tested or calibrated against defined acceptance criteria.

7. Inspection
   - Final inspection verifies conformity to customer and internal requirements.

8. Release & Shipment
   - Only approved products are released and shipped.
