## External Issues

SynTemp Vision monitors external factors including:

- Supply chain availability of electronic components, sensors, and materials
- Regulatory and compliance requirements applicable to our products
- Customer-specific technical and contractual requirements
- Industry technology advancements in sensing, control, and embedded systems
- Economic conditions impacting material cost and availability
- Cybersecurity risks affecting data and system integrity

## Internal Issues

Internal factors considered include:

- Technical competence and availability of personnel
- Manufacturing capacity and resource availability
- Infrastructure reliability (equipment, tools, software systems)
- Supplier dependency and single-source risks
- Process consistency and documentation control
- Rapid product or specification changes

## Review Method

Context is reviewed at least annually during Management Review or when significant business changes occur.

## Responsibility

Top Management is responsible for maintaining awareness of relevant internal and external issues.
