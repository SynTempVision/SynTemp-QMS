# 4.3 Determining the Scope of the Quality Management System

## Scope Statement

The Quality Management System of SynTemp Vision applies to the design, manufacture, assembly, verification, and distribution of thermocouples and related sensing assemblies.

## Applicability

All requirements of ISO 9001:2015 are applicable unless specifically excluded and justified.

## Exclusions

If Design and Development (Clause 8.3) is excluded:

Design and development activities are excluded from this QMS as products are manufactured to predefined customer specifications and no proprietary design activity is performed.
