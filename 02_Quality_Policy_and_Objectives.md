---

## 5.1 Leadership Commitment

Top Management is accountable for the effectiveness of the Quality Management System and ensures adequate resources, customer focus, and continual improvement.

Management participates in Management Review and approves policies and objectives.

---

## 5.2 Quality Policy

SynTemp is committed to providing high-quality temperature measurement instrumentation and engineered solutions that meet customer and regulatory requirements.

We achieve this by:

- Operating in accordance with defined specifications
- Maintaining an effective ISO 9001-based QMS
- Controlling nonconforming outputs
- Maintaining documented information
- Promoting competence and accountability
- Continually improving our processes

---

## 5.3 Roles and Responsibilities

Top Management:
- Ensures QMS effectiveness
- Provides necessary resources
- Approves policies and objectives

Quality Manager:
- Maintains QMS documentation
- Oversees audits and corrective actions

Operations Personnel:
- Follow work order requirements
- Prevent release of nonconforming product

---

## 6.2 Quality Objectives

SynTemp establishes measurable objectives including:

- ≥ 95% on-time delivery
- ≤ 2% internal nonconforming output
- Timely response to customer complaints
- Annual completion of internal audit

Objectives are monitored and reviewed during Management Review.
